Yale Astronomy Galaxy Lunch

Format/Topic

Galaxy Lunch meets Wednesdays noon-1pm in JWG 164. We discuss recent papers about galaxy formation/evolution based on votes on our Vox Charta page. We also host talks for the last 30 minutes. If you are giving a talk, please plan for a 20 minute talk, with roughly 10 minutes of questions and discussion during and after your talk.

If you are visiting Yale and would like to give a Galaxy Lunch talk, please contact Erik Tollerud with the date you will be at Yale (please check the schedule below to ensure that Wednesday is available).
